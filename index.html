Technologies We Use at Shop2Host
Front-End Technologies:
React:

We use React, a popular tool, to build websites that are responsive and easy to navigate. It helps create interactive pages that respond quickly to user actions.

Angular:

For more complex websites, we use Angular, a powerful framework that allows us to build large, feature-rich websites that work smoothly and efficiently.

Bootstrap:

Bootstrap helps us design websites that look great on all devices, from phones to desktops. With its ready-made styles and components, we can develop websites quickly and consistently.

Hosting Services:
Private Hosting with 99.9% Uptime:

Your website will be hosted on our private servers, which ensures it’s almost always up and running. We guarantee 99.9% uptime, meaning your site will experience very little downtime.

VPS (Virtual Private Server):

For customers who need more control and power, we offer VPS hosting. This means you’ll have your own virtual server, offering better security and performance than shared hosting.

SSL Certificates:

Every website hosted with us comes with a free SSL certificate. This ensures that all data transferred between your site and its visitors is encrypted and secure, protecting sensitive information like payment details.

LiteSpeed Server:

Our hosting runs on LiteSpeed servers, known for their high performance and speed. LiteSpeed is faster and more efficient than traditional web servers, ensuring that your website loads quickly and handles traffic spikes with ease.

Advanced Security Features:

Our hosting includes top-notch security features like firewalls, regular malware scans, and automatic backups to protect your data and keep your site secure.

Back-End Technologies:
Node.js:

We use Node.js to manage the back end of your website, handling everything from data processing to server-side scripting. Node.js is known for its speed and efficiency, ensuring your site runs smoothly.

MongoDB and SQL:

We use both MongoDB and SQL to manage your website’s databases. MongoDB is great for handling large amounts of unstructured data, while SQL is ideal for structured data like customer information and product inventories.

Comprehensive Security:

Our team employs advanced security measures to protect your site from threats, ensuring that your business and customer data are always safe.
