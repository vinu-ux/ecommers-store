Ideal for Growing Businesses

The Hero Plan is tailored for businesses that are expanding and need more storage, enhanced features, and multiple options to manage customer interactions effectively. This plan balances affordability and advanced functionality, making it perfect for scaling up.

What’s Included:

Free .com Domain NameEstablish a strong online presence with a professional .com domain included at no additional cost, giving your business a global appeal.

20GB of Storage SpaceAmple storage space to host a larger number of products, media files, and more complex content, supporting your growing business needs.

Monthly Renewal at ₹1499Enjoy a reduced renewal rate that helps maintain cost efficiency as you continue to grow your business online.

99.9% Uptime GuaranteeMaintain a high level of reliability with our uptime guarantee, ensuring your website is accessible almost all the time.

Free SSL Certificate: Protect your website and customer data with a free SSL certificate, providing a secure and trustworthy environment for online transactions.
