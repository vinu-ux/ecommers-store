Lite Plan - ₹999/year

Perfect for Small Businesses and Startups
The Lite Plan is designed to provide an affordable solution for businesses that are just starting out or need a basic online presence. With essential features included, this plan is a great way to establish your website and begin building your brand without a large upfront investment.

What’s Included:

Free .in Domain Name Get a professional-looking .in domain name included at no extra cost. This helps your business stand out and makes your website easy to find.

5GB of Storage Space:Ideal for hosting a basic website with product images, text, and essential media files. This storage capacity suits small-scale operations and startups.

Monthly Renewal at ₹499 Continue your online presence with a budget-friendly renewal cost. After the initial setup, the monthly renewal fee drops to ₹499, making it easy to maintain your website.

1 Business Email AddressCreate a business email (e.g., info@yourdomain.in) to communicate professionally with your clients and build trust with customers.

Limited Resources for Basic Needs This plan provides the necessary resources to manage essential e-commerce or informational website functions, perfect for sites with low to moderate traffic.

Single Payment Gateway Accept payments online securely with one payment gateway, simplifying transactions for you and your customers.

99.9% Uptime Guarantee High uptime reliability ensures your website is almost always online, minimizing downtime and maintaining customer trust.

Extra Benefits:

Free SSL Certificate: Protect your website and customer data with a free SSL certificate, enhancing security and trust.

Easy-to-Use Website Builder: Build your website with no technical skills required. Our user-friendly builder and customizable templates make creating a professional website easy and quick.

Logo and Heading Customization: Personalize your website by editing the logo and heading directly from the admin settings. Tailor your site’s appearance to match your brand identity effortlessly.

24/7 Live Chat Support: Our dedicated support team is available round-the-clock to assist you with any queries or issues. Whether you need help setting up your website or troubleshooting, our live chat is there to provide instant assistance.

Basic Analytics: Gain insights into your website’s performance with basic analytics tools. Track visitor statistics and understand user behavior to make informed business decisions.

New Entrepreneurs and Startups: If you’re launching a new business and need an affordable, reliable website, the Lite Plan is the perfect starting point.
