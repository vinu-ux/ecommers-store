Lite Plan - ₹999/year

Perfect for startups and small businesses that are just getting started online. This plan provides the essentials needed to create a professional and secure online presence.

Detailed Features:

5GB Storage:Sufficient storage space for small product catalogs, a limited number of high-resolution images, and basic website functionalities. Ideal for businesses just starting to sell online with a moderate number of products.

Free .in Domain: Includes a complimentary .in domain to establish a local online presence. A domain name helps build credibility and makes it easy for customers to find your website. This is especially useful for businesses targeting an Indian audience.

Limited Email Access (Up to 5 Addresses): Create up to 5 professional email addresses (e.g., info@yourstore.in) to maintain a professional line of communication with customers and partners. This feature helps you keep customer interactions organized and enhances trust.

Basic Website Design: Access to a variety of starter templates that are simple, modern, and easy to customize without any coding knowledge. This allows you to set up your website quickly while still maintaining a professional appearance.

2 Admin Accounts: Allows you to have two different login credentials for managing your website. Perfect for a business owner and a co-manager or employee. Each admin can handle different aspects of the website, from updating products to managing customer inquiries.

SSL Certificate: Included SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificate ensures that all data transferred between your website and your customers is encrypted and secure. This is crucial for protecting customer information and building trust.

Basic Support: Access to Shop2Host's help center, online resources, and email support. Suitable for addressing basic setup queries and technical issues.

Who Should Choose the Lite Plan?
The Lite Plan is ideal for individuals or small businesses that are just starting out and need a simple, secure, and cost-effective online presence. This plan covers all the basics required to launch and run a small e-commerce website effectively.

Hero Plan - ₹2499/year

A comprehensive plan designed for growing businesses that need more resources, enhanced features, and greater flexibility to handle increased traffic and a larger product range.

Detailed Features:

20GB Storage:Provides ample space to host a larger number of products, high-quality images, videos, and other content that can enhance the user experience. Perfect for medium-sized catalogs and businesses looking to scale.

Free .com Domain:A .com domain helps establish a more global presence and is often perceived as more professional and credible. This is ideal for businesses that plan to reach a broader audience beyond India.

Unlimited Email Access:No limits on the number of professional email accounts you can create. This is beneficial for growing businesses that require multiple departments (e.g., sales@, support@, info@) to communicate with customers and partners.

Enhanced Website Design Options:Access to a wider range of templates with advanced customization options. You can tailor your website to reflect your brand identity, making it stand out from competitors. Drag-and-drop editors make customization easy and intuitive.

5 Admin Accounts:More admin accounts mean more team members can manage various aspects of the website, such as inventory, customer service, marketing, and analytics. This is ideal for businesses with multiple employees involved in different areas of operations.

Integrated Payment Gateways (Razorpay, Paytm):Secure and reliable payment integration with popular payment gateways like Razorpay and Paytm. This makes it easy for customers to pay using their preferred method, enhancing user experience and trust.

Chatbot Integration:Automatically handle customer inquiries and support with a built-in chatbot feature. This reduces the need for a large customer support team and ensures quick responses to customer questions, improving customer satisfaction.

Mobile Optimization:Ensures that your website looks great and functions smoothly on mobile devices, which is crucial since a significant portion of online shopping is done on smartphones.

Priority Support:Faster response times for any technical issues or questions you may have. Access to dedicated support channels ensures that your problems are resolved quickly, minimizing downtime and disruption.

SEO Tools:Advanced SEO tools help optimize your website for search engines, making it easier for potential customers to find you online. These tools include keyword optimization, meta tag editing, and analytics tracking to monitor your SEO performance.

Who Should Choose the Hero Plan?
The Hero Plan is perfect for small to medium-sized businesses that are experiencing growth and need more storage, flexibility, and professional features. It is ideal for businesses that want to enhance their online presence, expand their customer base, and improve customer engagement.

The ultimate plan for established businesses and enterprises that require the most power, customization, and support to manage a large-scale online operation.

Detailed Features:

Unlimited Storage:No restrictions on storage space, allowing you to upload unlimited products, high-resolution images, videos, and extensive website content without worrying about running out of space. This is crucial for businesses with large inventories or content-heavy websites.

Unlimited Email Accounts:Create as many email addresses as needed for your entire team. This is important for large businesses with multiple departments and team members who need their own professional email addresses.

Unlimited Admin Access:No limit on the number of administrators who can manage the website. This is ideal for large teams where multiple employees need access to various functions of the e-commerce platform, such as order processing, inventory management, and marketing.

Tailor every aspect of your website’s design, layout, and functionality to perfectly align with your brand’s needs and vision.
